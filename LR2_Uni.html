History of ASOIU

On November 10, 1887, the Baku City Duma issued a decision to establish a technical school in Baku. The technical school was transformed into “Baku low-level technical school” in 1896. During 1896-1905, 50 students graduated from the Mechanical Department and 55 from the Construction Department of the school.
